Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

VidyaSankar Panchangam does not collect, store, or share any personal information about its users.

Location Data

This app requests access to your device's location (geolocation) only to provide accurate Panchangam and Lagna timings for your current location. Location access is required for the app to function correctly.

Your location is never stored or shared with any third party. The location is used only within the app and only with your explicit consent.

Consent

When you use the app, you will be asked to grant permission for location access. If you do not grant permission, the app will use a default location (Hyderabad) for calculations.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at me@panchang.am.in.
